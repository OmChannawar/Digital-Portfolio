My Projects

A selection of projects that highlight my passion for technology, problem-solving, and hands-on innovation. Each one reflects my journey of learning and applying concepts in real-world scenarios.

Wireless Power Transfer

My very first technical project, inspired during a Grade 9 online session on the fundamentals of electricity. I explored the concept of wireless energy transmission without physical connectors and followed a guided tutorial to successfully build a working mini wireless power transfer device at home. This project sparked my curiosity for practical applications of science and technology.

MystiCode – Secure PIN & Code Generator

A lightweight, offline web app for generating secure, customizable codes and PINs instantly. No sign-ups, no tracking, and 100% private — users can choose length, characters, and copy results with one click. MystiCode is designed for speed, simplicity, and security, making it a practical everyday tool for anyone.

Line Following Robot

A beginner-friendly robotics project built with Arduino, IR sensors, and DC motors. This robot follows a path marked on the ground, teaching the principles of autonomous navigation and conditional programming logic. It provided me with hands-on experience in electronics and coding.

Obstacle Avoider Robot

An autonomous robot capable of detecting and avoiding obstacles using ultrasonic sensors. The project improved my understanding of sensor integration, logical decision-making, and real-world robotics applications. It demonstrated how intelligent systems adapt to dynamic environments — a foundation for advanced robotics.
